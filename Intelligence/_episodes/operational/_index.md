# Operational episodes

The agent's own verb runs — what it routed, why, what failed, what
worked. Captured at the end of every `consolidate`/`query`/`refine`/
`evaluate` run. Recall pulls similar past runs as **exemplars** before
the agent acts, so routing/retrieval decisions compound over time.
