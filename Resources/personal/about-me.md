# About me

Replace this file with a first-person description of who you are. The agent treats
this as a prescriptive source — it preserves your wording rather than paraphrasing.
Write in the voice you want reflected back.

Suggested sections:

- **Who I am** — role, background, what I care about
- **How I work** — decision-making style, collaboration preferences, communication norms
- **Current focus** — what I'm working on and why it matters right now
- **Tools and context** — key tools, systems, or people relevant to my work
