# Writing rules

Replace this file with your personal writing guidelines. The agent treats this as a
prescriptive source and follows these rules when generating any text on your behalf.

Suggested sections:

- **Tone** — formal/informal, warm/direct, first/third person
- **Style** — sentence length, active vs. passive voice, use of lists
- **Vocabulary** — preferred terms, words to avoid, domain jargon conventions
- **Formatting** — heading style, punctuation preferences, how to handle emphasis
- **Things I dislike** — specific patterns, clichés, or habits to actively avoid
